Design System Inspiration of Duno

A B2B compliance and onboarding platform whose marketing surfaces lean almost entirely on a single signature: hand-painted watercolor mountain-and-sky illustrations spanning the page top, a mid-page sky-blue callout band, and the page-base footer. Type sets at extreme display sizes; the canvas stays pure white; the only chromatic action color is a deep warm near-black.

01 · Color Palette

Monochrome with one painted accent

The system is built from a pure white canvas, a single deep warm near-black for actions and dark slabs, and one sky-blue surface used exclusively on the mid-page callout band. The watercolor illustration carries every chromatic moment beyond that.

08 · Elevation & Depth

Tier shifts, not stacks

No stacked shadows. Elevation reads through colour-block contrast — the dark slab against pure-white canvas is the system's only emphasis device.

Level 0 — flush
No shadow, no border. Default for body, color blocks.
Level 1 — hairline
1px hairline divider. Footer cols, FAQ rows, nav underline.
Level 2 — soft float
Reserved for the floating press-quote and overlapping testimonial slab.

Touch Targets

Top-right CTA pill renders at 40px height (48px on hero placements) satisfying WCAG AAA touch-target guidance. FAQ tap targets extend the full row width.

Collapsing Strategy

  • Top nav: horizontal menu + CTA at desktop → hamburger + CTA at ≤ 810 px; menu opens to a full-screen canvas overlay.
  • Hero: headline overlaid on watercolor at desktop → headline below the illustration on mobile.
  • Case-card / feature-tile grids: 3-up → 2-up at 999 px → 1-up at 720 px.
  • Footer: 4-column → 2-column at 810 px → single accordion at 720 px.
